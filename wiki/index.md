# Munro

A clean, map-first hiking tracker for UK peak bagging.

Munro lets users view the UK's major mountain and fell lists on a
topographic-style map, mark peaks as bagged, track progress by national park
or hill list, and export a shareable image showing their completed peaks.

The first version stays deliberately simple: a flawless tracker, not a
social network, route planner, GPX library or navigation tool.

## How it feels

Users open the app, choose a hill list or national park, and see all
relevant peaks plotted on a dark, minimal topographic map. Unbagged peaks
appear muted grey. Bagged peaks illuminate in a soft green accent. The user
can click a peak, view basic information, mark it as bagged, and see their
progress update.

The app should feel like a premium outdoor logbook with a technical map
aesthetic.

## Where it runs

Munro is being developed into a single product accessible **via the web, on
iPhone and on Android** — one design language and one record on every
screen. See [Platforms](platforms.md) for the strategy.
